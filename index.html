Welcome to Skyline!

Skyline is a Nintendo Switch Emulator designed for use on Android ARM64 devices.
The project is currently within the early stages of development, although you can still install and test it as long as your device has the following minimum requirements.



  • 64-bit ARM processor

    Most Snapdragon processors that are 64-bit, will work fine. We recommend a Snapdragon 835 or greater. Although, you can run it on a lower end processor (assuming it is 64-bit), it is not recommended.
  • 4 GB of RAM

    An Android device with preferably 4 GB of RAM or greater. Some phones with 3 GB of RAM or 2 GB, may or may not work.
  • Something to Test

    Try testing your favorite Nintendo Switch homebrew! It may work, as long as it's an NRO.
